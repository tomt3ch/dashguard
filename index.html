Lens selection

Start camera first to see available lenses.
Use 1x (main) for best all-around range. 2x or 3x telephoto sees signs earliest — ideal for highway speeds. Avoid ultrawide (0.5x) — distant objects are too small to detect.

Detection sensitivity

Lower catches more distant signs but risks false positives. 30–40% recommended. 25% for telephoto (less noise at range).

Focal length calibration

Auto-set when selecting a lens. Adjust if distance estimates feel too near or too far.
